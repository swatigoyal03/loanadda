Types of Home Loan

There is a home loan for every requirement, take a look:

  • Ready Property Purchase Loans: These are for buying a new home.
  • Land Purchase Loans: These loans are for buying a new plot either as an investment or to construct a house.
  • Home Construction Loans: Opt for a home construction loan, if you plan to build a house over an already owned land.
  • Home Improvement Loans: These loans are applicable when you want to renovate or re-build your house.
  • Balance Transfer Loans: All banks are competing to bring the best rates to the customer. The customer can make use of the lower interest rate facilities to move his existing loan to another service provider at a better rate. As per the RBI guideline, there is no longer any pre-payment penalty for foreclosure of the loan.
  • NRI Home Loans: Specially designed for the benefit of non-resident Indians. The maximum tenure is 15 years and the rate of interest is a little higher than existing rates. The paperwork is also a little longer and complicated.
